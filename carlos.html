About the Company

A Legacy of Technical Expertise and Leadership

CO Enterprises is a general contracting company founded in January 2026. The company specializes in commercial, industrial, and residential construction, including new construction, renovation, retrofitting, mechanical, electrical, and HVAC services.

CO Enterprises manages every stage of the construction process, working in partnership with prime contractors. The company oversees project scheduling, procurement, and execution with a strong commitment to quality and efficiency.

Our success is driven by experience, dedication, and professional management.

Our Mission and Vision

CO Enterprises is a locally oriented company committed to delivering high-quality workmanship. Our mission is to provide exceptional construction services that create value for our clients and the community.
